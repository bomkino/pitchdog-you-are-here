# Support

For reproducible defects and product improvements, use the issue templates.

For security vulnerabilities, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). For conduct concerns or private project material, use the private contact route on [pitch.dog](https://pitch.dog/). Do not paste unreleased pitch copy, contact information, or client material into a public issue.

This is an open-source creative tool, not an emergency, legal, finance, rights-clearance, or mental-health service.
