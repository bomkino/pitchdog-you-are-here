# Names, marks, and forks

The source is open. Brand identity is not ownerless.

`pitch.dog`, the pitch.dog logo, and the official presentation of this project identify the original work. The software and asset licenses do not grant permission to claim endorsement, impersonate the official service, or use those marks as the primary identity of a modified version.

You may truthfully say your fork is based on this project, link here for attribution, and show the official interface or mark when reviewing, teaching, or documenting it in a way permitted by law.

If you distribute or host a modified version, give it a distinct name and visual identity. A clear note such as “Forked from a pitch.dog project; not affiliated with pitch.dog” is useful.

The approved logo duplicate remains in the repository because the official build needs it and the project should be reproducible. Do not treat that file as a general-purpose logo pack.
