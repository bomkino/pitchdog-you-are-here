# Product contract

## The job

Identify the smallest useful next move in a live pitch situation before defaulting to a deck.

## Inputs

The tool may use lane, stage, immediate goal, recipient, access route, relationship, current request, readiness, evidence, rights, authority, budget, timing, and delivery conditions. Quick mode uses five high-leverage choices. Expert mode asks only after consent.

## Outputs

- one primary next move;
- a short reason;
- what to bring or establish first;
- what can wait;
- assumptions and limits;
- an optional path into the deeper map;
- a local download preserving the move, finish line, reasons, expert context, parked work, and limits.

## Boundaries

You Are Here does not diagnose people, assess danger, promise access, validate legal or financial claims, or decide whether a project is good. It routes pitch work using supplied structured facts. Current written requirements and qualified specialist advice outrank generic tool guidance.

## Privacy

Selections and results remain in the browser. The production tool has no account, database, analytics, upload, or runtime AI call.
